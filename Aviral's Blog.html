Question of the day


What is life all about?
  • A healthy liver
  • Sex
  • a les paul
  • A mere distraction

If you feel that the answer is "A mere distraction" then you are WRONG.

Life is much more positive than being a distraction.Its what you ware willing to fill it with. Like a bowl you can put in positive hobbies and skills.
You can put negative things as well but that's just boring.
Imagine someone who is always sad.If he is happy by doing an activity then why not do it.Brain is a very important tool.If left without any work it creates work by thinking and then overthinking.

Ask yourself these questions

  1. Am I sad?
  2. Why am I sad?
  3. What makes me happy?

Now simply reverse the order of the question to-

  1. What makes me happy?
  2. After doing that thing,Am I still sad?

But the 'I' point is are you doing everything you can to be happy?

Remember it all starts with you. Take care of yourself.If you cannot do that then you will always keep facing the same problems in life.
Learn to take a break.Find out what truely excites you and then Go for it

Over the years I have been in and out of feeling low and completely unmotivated.In fact I am at that place right about now.Having a routine helps me to have perspective.
